# Intimate — Relationship Training

A relationship and intimacy skills trainer powered by AI.

100 personas across different nationalities, ages (20–40), attachment styles, educational backgrounds, and life histories. Practice real conversations across 6 scenarios and get coaching feedback.

## Features

- 100 unique personas with full psychological profiles
- 6 training scenarios: first date, deep conversation, conflict, opening up, commitment talk, long distance
- AI-powered roleplay with authentic attachment style behavior
- Coaching feedback with scores across 4 dimensions
- Progress tracking across sessions

## Deploy

Static HTML — no build step needed. Just deploy `index.html`.
